Timeline

1957 Sputnik 1: First artificial satellite (USSR) 1961 Yuri Gagarin: First human in space (USSR) 1969 Apollo 11: Humans walk on Moon (USA) 1971 Salyut 1: First space station (USSR) 1977 Voyager 1 & 2: Grand Tour of outer planets (NASA) 1981 First Space Shuttle launch (Columbia) 1990 Hubble Telescope deployed 1998 ISS construction begins 2012 Curiosity lands on Mars 2022 James Webb Telescope operational 2023 India lands near Moon's south pole (Chandrayaan-3) 2024 Artemis II: Crewed Moon flyby Dragonfly: Nuclear drone to Titan (2034)
